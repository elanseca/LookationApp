About

    Do you wish to buy a product in Tuy? Are you unsure if the store exists? Would you want an easier way of searching a store?

    Look-ation offers a convenient way of locating an establishment within the vicinity of Tuy "Bayan" Proper with using only your smartphone. The application provclasses a store's information, contact details, map location and what it can offer to the resclassents of Tuy and also travelers who wish to know available establishments in town.

    The developers of Look-ation aim to give more awareness to local and growing establishments located in Tuy, Batangas.
